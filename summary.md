# Sports News Report

## 1. Suryakumar Yadav Hails Axar Patel as India's 'Unsung Hero'

India's T20I captain, Suryakumar Yadav, has lauded all-rounder Axar Patel as the team's "unsung hero" for his consistent and impactful performances across all formats (T20Is, ODIs, Tests). Yadav praised Patel's composure, reliability, and willingness to bowl in crucial situations, including powerplays and death overs. Axar Patel is set to play in the fourth T20I against Australia on November 6.

## 2. Ayodhya Premier League 2025 Set to Begin

The inaugural Ayodhya Premier League (APL) 2025 will commence on November 9 at the Dr. Bhimrao Ambedkar International Sports Complex in Ayodhya.

**Key Details:**
*   **Dates:** November 9 - November 26, 2025
*   **Venue:** Dr. Bhimrao Ambedkar International Sports Complex, Ayodhya
*   **Format:** T20, 8 teams, single round-robin league stage (28 matches). Top 4 teams advance to playoffs.
*   **Playoffs:** Semifinals on November 24, Final on November 26.
*   **Live Streaming:** Dafabet website.

## 3. Suryakumar Yadav Seeks AB de Villiers' Advice on ODI Success

India's T20I captain, Suryakumar Yadav, expressed his desire to improve his ODI cricket performance and revealed he wants to ask South African legend AB de Villiers how he managed to master both ODI and T20I formats. Yadav admitted struggling to adapt to the 50-over format and considers the next 3-4 years crucial for his ODI ambitions, with an eye on the 2027 World Cup.

## 5. Jemimah Rodrigues Responds to Sunil Gavaskar's Duet Promise

Following India's triumph in the Women's World Cup 2025, cricketer Jemimah Rodrigues has enthusiastically accepted cricket legend Sunil Gavaskar's offer to sing a duet. Gavaskar had previously promised to sing with Rodrigues, who plays the guitar, if India won the trophy. Rodrigues posted a video stating she's "ready with my guitar" and hopes Gavaskar is "ready with your mic," rekindling memories of their impromptu performance at a past BCCI event.
